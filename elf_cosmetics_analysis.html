Measurement gap — what we cannot currently see
Attribution blind spot: e.l.f. runs TikTok Shop, DTC, Amazon, and mass retail simultaneously with no unified cross-channel view of where each sale originates.
TikTok Shop siloed: GMV Max contribution cannot be compared to paid media ROAS — they live in separate dashboards, making budget allocation guesswork.
Scale headroom unknown: Without incrementality forecasting, e.l.f. cannot confidently know when scaling TikTok spend starts hitting diminishing returns.

Strategic recommendations
1
Unify attribution across all channels — implement full-funnel measurement to connect TikTok Shop, DTC, Amazon, and retail into a single performance view with consistent ROAS methodology.
2
Deploy Beam incremental forecasting — benchmark suggests ~40% spend headroom before diminishing returns on GMV Max; e.l.f.'s scale likely has significant untapped room given early-stage TikTok Shop adoption.
3
Combine Smart+ web ads with TikTok Shop — Data shows brands running both see 85% higher conversion growth and 28% better new customer growth YoY vs. single-channel.
4
Leverage first-party data for retargeting — e.l.f.'s 5M+ UGC audience and 7.7M TikTok followers represent a large retargetable pool; privacy-safe measurement enables smarter lookalike and suppression audiences.
