01 — What is Fospha?

Your Measurement Operating System

Fospha is the Measurement Operating System for retail commerce. It solves a problem every growing brand faces: knowing which ads are actually driving growth — not just the ones that got the last click. Using a Daily Marketing Mix Model, Fospha gives you a single, independent view of performance across every channel, every day.

📊

Not last-click attribution

We model the full customer journey — from the first TikTok impression to the final checkout — so every channel gets fair credit for its role.

⚡

Daily, not monthly

Unlike traditional measurement that lags weeks behind, Fospha refreshes every single day so your team can act on fresh data, fast.

🔒

Privacy-first by design

No third-party cookies, no user-level tracking. Built to meet GDPR, CCPA, and iOS14+ standards — measurement that works in a privacy-first world.
